Welcome to my Portfolio!

I am Anurag Anirudha Kelkar, a Computer Science Master's student at the University of Florida with a GPA of 3.89/4.0. With a strong foundation in Data Structures, Algorithms, Distributed Systems, and Software Engineering, I enjoy solving complex problems and building efficient, scalable software solutions.

I have hands-on experience in full-stack development, DevOps, and cloud technologies, having worked as a Graduate Student Assistant (Web Developer) at the University of Florida, where I developed Python/Flask APIs for OCR processing and optimized React-based frontends to enhance user experience. My industry experience includes two internships at Securonix India, where I worked on CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes microservices, and auto-scaling solutions, improving system performance and deployment efficiency.

I am proficient in Python, Go, JavaScript, and TypeScript, with expertise in frameworks such as React.js, Node.js, and Spring Boot. My projects include a Workout Tracker App using React, Go, and PostgreSQL, a News Aggregator with NLP-based clustering, and a Library Management System using Red-Black Trees for efficient book management.

With a strong interest in cloud computing, DevOps, and distributed systems, I am eager to contribute to innovative solutions that drive performance and scalability. Always looking for opportunities to learn, collaborate, and solve impactful problems, I am open to roles in software development, DevOps, and cloud engineering.
